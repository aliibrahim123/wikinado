welcome

Tornado is a violently rotating column of air that extends from a thunderstorm to the ground, creating a distinctive funnel-shaped cloud. Resulted from infinite factors and conected to uncountable side effect with unbeatable destructive power, it is important to have a fulfilling source of information about this natural disaster.

and for that we introduce to you the "WIKINADO", a fully featured mini wiki about tornados that cover every domain of tornados in a simple plain english format.
